Welcome to Game Setup: Board Game Cafe

About Us


Game Setup is Leeds' most popular board game cafe. Our knowledgable and enthusiastic team are available to answer any questions or to make recommendations. We aim to create a friendly, welcoming space for seasoned gamers or those new to the hobby, where they can explore our catalogue of games. As well as games we also serve a wide variety of drinks including craft beer, coffees & teas plus a refined food menu. You are welcome to purchase any games that you enjoy playing or we can work with you to source a copy of something that you are after. This service is unfortunately only available in the cafe at present but we are working with our developers to build a function within this website where you can both browse and purchase games that we stock. You're welcome to pop into the cafe to make an enquiry or for an easy way to secure a table for your friends or family you can use our booking function by clicking here. Please feel free to browse our website to see some of the games we have in our catalogue or check out our instore food menu.
